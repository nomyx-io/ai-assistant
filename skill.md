To create a newsreport:
1. Search the web for news about the target topic. Use News search, GDELT search, google search. Gather a list of articles to look at.
2. Pick the most relevant articles from the article list you have gathered. 
3. Read each one of the articles you’ve selected, noting relevant facts and figures.
4. Generate a detailed synopsis of the articles. Use a professional tone meant for verbal delivery.
5. Speak the news report out lout using the say tool
